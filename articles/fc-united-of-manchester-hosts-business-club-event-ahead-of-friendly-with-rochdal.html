Rochdale AFC Prepares for FC United Clash

Rochdale AFC gears up for an away match against FC United, aiming to secure vital points in the league.

By Rochdale Daily Newsdesk

Rochdale AFC is set to face FC United in an away match this weekend. The team is looking to build on recent performances and secure crucial points in the league.

Manager Jim Bentley has expressed confidence in his squad's ability to compete effectively against FC United. He highlighted the importance of maintaining momentum as the season progresses.

Fans are encouraged to support the team as they travel to Manchester for the fixture. The match is expected to draw a significant crowd, with both clubs having a strong following.

Kick-off is scheduled for 3pm on Saturday, and supporters can follow live updates through the club's official channels.
