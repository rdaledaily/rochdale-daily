Food banks and pantries in Rochdale borough

Every food bank, pantry, food club and soup kitchen we can identify across Rochdale, Heywood, Middleton, Littleborough, Milnrow and Smallbridge — with addresses, opening times, costs and what you need to bring.

Ring before you travel

Opening times, costs and referral rules change often, and some of these services run on donated stock. A wasted journey is the last thing you need, so please check with the provider before setting off.

Where a listing says a voucher or referral is needed, you will usually need one before you arrive. Citizens Advice, your school, health visitor, GP, social worker or housing officer can normally issue one. Rochdale Borough Council’s Discretionary Crisis Fund can also help in an emergency.

If you have no food today

Ring Rochdale Borough Council on 01706 647474 and ask about the Crisis and Resilience Fund, or contact Rochdale Foodbank on 01706 713701. Both can point you to same-day help.

If you are struggling to cope, Samaritans are free to call on 116 123 at any hour.
